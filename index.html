ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Jason Leibowitz and I am a junior web developer, originally from Virginia Beach, living in Brooklyn, NY. I recently completed General Assembly's 12-week Web Development Immersive where I learned fundamental front and back-end languages, programming methodologies, and best-practices. I'm now eager for the opportunity to work for a passionate, team-oriented tech company in New York as a junior developer.

I have always been interested in technology and am a voracious and quick learner. I have loved learning programming and the ability to create applications has rounded out my technological skill set and opened up whole new worlds for me.

I graduated New York University with a degree in History in 2009 and worked in Jewish non-profit for four and a half years. I thoroughly enjoyed giving back to the community and helping those in need, but am now excited to start a new chapter.
