About Me

Hi there! My name is Jason Leibowitz and I am a web developer, originally from Virginia Beach, living in Brooklyn, NY. I am currently working at TodayTix as a Software Engineer. Previously, I worked at Reserve and a digital agency, Tigerspike, and graduated from General Assembly's 12-week Web Development Immersive where I learned fundamental front and back-end languages, programming methodologies, and best-practices.

I graduated New York University with a degree in History and worked in Jewish non-profit for four and a half years.
